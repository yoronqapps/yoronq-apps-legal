TradeRemind

Privacy Policy  |  Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Developer: Yoronq Apps  |  Contact: [email protected]

TradeRemind ("the App", "we", "our") is a personal inactivity tracker designed for prop firm traders. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. It applies to all users worldwide and is written to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), UK GDPR, California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and other applicable laws.

1. Information We Collect

TradeRemind is designed with privacy as a priority. Almost all data you enter never leaves your device.

Data you enter — stored locally on your device only:

  • Account names and prop firm labels
  • Account sizes and equity figures
  • Trade logs: pairs, profit/loss, dates, and notes
  • Notification and reminder preferences
  • App settings such as theme and PIN lock status

This data is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data.

Data collected automatically by third-party SDKs (free-tier users only):

If you are a free-tier user, the App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect: Advertising ID, IP address, general device information, and ad interaction data. PRO users do not see advertisements and AdMob data collection does not apply to them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the App's core features: tracking accounts, analytics, and inactivity reminders
  • To remember your preferences (theme, PIN, notification timing)
  • To display relevant advertisements to free-tier users via Google AdMob

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (EU/UK Users)

  • Contract performance: Processing your locally-stored data is necessary to provide the App's features.
  • Legitimate interests: Displaying ads to free-tier users to sustain the App's development.
  • Consent: Where required by law (e.g. personalised advertising under GDPR), we seek your explicit consent via an in-app prompt. You may withdraw consent at any time.

4. Advertising and Consent

Before any advertising data is collected, the App presents a consent prompt in compliance with Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) requirements and applicable privacy laws including GDPR and US state privacy laws. You may choose personalised ads, non-personalised ads, or manage your preferences at any time.

For full details of how Google processes advertising data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your trading data is stored locally on your device using Android's SQLite database. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it. We recommend enabling your device's screen lock and using the App's built-in PIN feature to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

All data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. We do not retain copies of your data on any server. Once you uninstall the App, information is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by us.

7. Your Rights

EU/UK users (GDPR/UK GDPR): Right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and to withdraw consent.

California users (CPRA): Right to know what is collected, right to delete, right to opt out of sale (we do not sell personal information), and right to non-discrimination.

Because your data is stored locally, you can exercise most rights directly in the App's Settings. For anything else, contact us at [email protected]. We respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

TradeRemind is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your locally-stored trading data does not leave your device and involves no international transfer by us. Google AdMob may transfer advertising data internationally under Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

10. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (advertising, free-tier users only) — policies.google.com/privacy

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via an in-app notice on your next launch. The Effective Date at the top will be updated accordingly.
